iOS systems：

  1. Click the "Edit" button in the upper right corner of the "Video" or "Picture" page.
  2. Click the "Add" button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Click on the "Choose from this album" TAB.
  4. On the displayed page, select the video or image to import.
  5. Click the "Cancel" button in the upper right corner to complete the file import operation.

macOS systems：

  1. Connect the device to your computer with a USB cable.
  2. Open "Finder" on your computer.
  3. Select your device (such as Xiaoming's iPhone) under "Locations" in the left column of "Finder". The device information will be displayed in the right column of "Finder".
  4. Select "Files" in the information bar of your device to find the "Multiple Players" application.
  5. Expand "Multiple Players", drag the video or picture in the desktop or folder into "Multiple Players", that is, the file import operation is complete.
  6. Close "Finder", restart the "Multiple Players" application on the device, and you can see the video or picture file that I imported.

Windows systems：

  1. Install "iTunes" on your computer and open it when the installation is complete.
  2. Connect the device to the computer with a USB data cable. At this time, select "Trust" in the device pop-up box.
  3. Click the device icon next to the right of "Music" under the menu bar of "iTunes" (the icon is small, please pay attention to finding it), and the relevant information of the device will be displayed.
  4. Click "Files Sharing" under "Settings" on the left of "iTunes", and relevant information will be displayed on the right.
  5. Click "Multiple Players" and the videos and pictures we already have will be displayed in "Documents" next to it.
  6. Drag the video or picture in the desktop or folder into the "Documents", that is, the file import operation is completed; You can also click "Add Files" at the bottom of "Documents" and select My file from the pop-up box to complete the import operation.
  7. Close "iTunes", restart the "Multiple Players" application on your device, and you can see the video or picture file we imported.
